Dive at Molasses Reef, Key Largo, 40ft depth, good viz.

Saw a ton of sergeant majors hanging around the reef structure, probably over a
hundred of them schooling. A few blue tangs grazing on algae, maybe 5-6.
One nurse shark resting under a ledge near the start of the dive. Also noticed
what I think was a big group of yellowtail damselfish near the coral heads,
hard to count exactly but definitely more than a dozen. Single spotted eagle
ray cruised by mid-dive.
